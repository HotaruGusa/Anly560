Data Visualization

There is some general data information I got from my datasets.

Top 10 country with beer consumption

There are the top 10 countries that drink alcohol in the world.
I use a pie chart to show this information to see if some countries have higher consumption than others.
They have similar consumption and we can see most countries like Russia are in the north. There may need alcohol to resist the cold.



The beer consumption in Russia

There is beer consumption in Russia based on different regions.
From this graph we can see beer consumption has clearly tended to increase from 1998 to 2010.
However, after 2010, the tendency has stopped and becomes decreasing.

  • Russia beer consumption


The beer consumption in different countries

There is beer consumption in the capital of different countries.
When we compare this graph to Russia’s, we can not see the significant tendency of the beer consumption.
This graph looks stationary.

  • Capital's beer consumption


The stock price in HongKong

It is also necessary to analyze the stock price of different beer companies.
This is the stock price of a beer company in HongKong. The data choose the highest stock price per day from 2019 to 2022.
We can see the stock increase from 2019 to 2011. However, it tends to decrease after 2021.



The stock price in Boston

Sam, Samuel Adams Boston Brewery is a famous beer company in Boston.
The graph shows the similar situation as HongKong’s beer company at the first.
However, when 2011 began, the stock price still changed a lot and tended to decrease.
