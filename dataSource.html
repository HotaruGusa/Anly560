Data Sources

There are many different dataframe I have collected in the internet or using API.

Two dataset from Kaggle:

The drinks.csv record the different type of alcohol consumption around the world in 2010.
Types include: beer, spirit, wine and total pure alcohol.
The unit is average serving sizes per person. https://www.kaggle.com/mysarahmadbhat/alcohol-consumption

The russia.csv record the different alcohol consumption in Russia from 1998 to 2016.
Types of alcohol include: wince, beer, vokda, champagne, brandy.
The unit is itres by year per capita. https://www.kaggle.com/dwdkills/alcohol-consumption-in-russia

  • drinks.csv
  • russia.csv


One dataset from ourworldindata.org:

The beer.csv collect the beer consumption in each country from 1961 to 2019.
The unit is litres per person. https://ourworldindata.org/alcohol-consumption

  • beer.csv


Two dataset from data.world:

The capital.csv collect the different types of alcohol consumption for each capital of the US state from 1977 to 2018.
Types include: beer, wine, spirit.
The unit is gallon per person. https://data.world/scuttlemonkey/apparent-per-capita-alcohol-consumption

  • capital.csv


One dataset from Yahoo Finance:

I use the Yahoo Finance API to get this dataset. This is a beer company stock located in China from 2019 to 2021. https://finance.yahoo.com/quote/0291.HK?p=0291.HK&.tsrc=fin-srch
